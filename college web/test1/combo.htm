IBSAR

Institute of Business Studies & Research is a business school located in the CBD Belapur district of Navi Mumbai and also at Baner Pune, India. IBSAR was established in 2005 in Mumbai and 2007 in Pune. It focuses on business programs for specific disciplines and places emphasis on training for the business world.
