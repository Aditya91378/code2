• The Misfit of Demon Academy

    • The anime has a powerful Demon Emperor who sacrificed himself in order to stop the war between humans and demons. He was reincarnated after 2000 years. The main character is also known as "Anos Voldigod". After reincarnating, he discovered that magic had been depleted in the world. Also he finds out that the history of the demon world also his name has been altered.He then tries to recover the truth behind the things that has happened in his absense.

  • Wise man's Grandchild

    • This Anime also has an character who is reincarnated in a magic world and becomes immensely strong .A strong mage named Merlin once known as the hero of a kingdom adopted an orphan who was actually the reincarnation of an average salary man with the past memories retained in the modern world.As the years pass, He displays unparalleled talent in both magic casting and martial arts, much to Merlin's constant amazement.
